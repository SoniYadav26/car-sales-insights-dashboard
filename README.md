# Car-Sales-Analysis-Dashboard(Interactive Dashboard creation using Power Bi)
## Project Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze car sales data and create an interactive dashboard using Power BI. This helps stakeholders understand sales performance, revenue trends, customer preferences, and regional performance to make better business decisions.

## Project Insight
- The company generated $371.2M YTD total sales in 2023 with a 23.6% YoY growth, indicating strong business expansion compared to the previous year.
- A total of 13.3K cars were sold YTD, reflecting a 19.73% increase from last year.
- The average selling price (ASP) remained stable at $28K, showing controlled pricing strategy despite growth.
- Certain body styles (SUVs and Hatchbacks) contribute significantly to overall revenue.
- Weekly sales trend analysis shows clear seasonal fluctuations with noticeable peak periods.
- Company-wise breakdown reveals that a few brands contribute disproportionately to total revenue.
- Regional dealer analysis highlights specific regions driving higher car volumes.
- MTD vs PYTD comparison helps monitor short-term performance against last yearâ€™s trend.

## Final Conclusion:
The Car Sales Dashboard demonstrates strong year-over-year growth in both revenue and volume. The business is expanding steadily while maintaining average price stability.
To further improve performance:
Focus marketing and inventory planning around high-performing body styles.
Strengthen presence in top-performing dealer regions.
Monitor weekly sales patterns to optimize promotional campaigns.
Use MTD vs PYTD insights to quickly detect performance deviations.
Overall, this dashboard enables data-driven decision-making through advanced time intelligence and dynamic KPI monitoring.
